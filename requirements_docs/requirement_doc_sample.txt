# Login and Checkout Functional Requirements

## Login Feature
- Users must be able to log in with email and password.
- Password must have at least 8 characters, one uppercase, and one special character.
- Show "Invalid credentials" for wrong password.
- Implement "Forgot Password" flow that sends reset link.

## Checkout Feature
- Users can add items to cart and proceed to checkout.
- Payment methods supported: Credit Card, PayPal.
- Address and phone validation must be mandatory before placing order.
- Confirmation email should be sent after successful payment.
